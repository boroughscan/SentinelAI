HOW IT WORKS

Three Steps. Zero Missed Warnings.

📡
STEP 01
MONITOR

Watches 4 critical spacecraft sensors — cabin temperature, oxygen levels, battery charge, and CO₂ concentration — every mission minute, continuously.

🧠
STEP 02
DETECT

An LSTM autoencoder neural network learns the signature of a healthy spacecraft. Any deviation from normal patterns triggers a reconstruction error spike above the calibrated threshold.

🚨
STEP 03
ALERT

SentinelAI fires a critical warning — with sensor identification, confidence score, and estimated time to failure — up to 110 minutes before a critical fault becomes unrecoverable.
